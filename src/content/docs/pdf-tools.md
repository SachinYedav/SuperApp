The **PDF Master** is your ultimate digital document assistant. Whether you need to combine reports, extract a single page, or convert a batch of images into a PDF, this tool handles it all instantly and securely.

> **Privacy Guarantee:** 100% Client-Side Processing. Your sensitive documents (like IDs, bank statements, or contracts) are processed entirely within your browser. **No files are ever uploaded to our servers.**

## üåü Key Features

* **Images to PDF:** Select multiple images (JPG, PNG) and compile them into a single, neat PDF document.
* **PDF to Images:** Extract every page of a PDF into high-quality images.
* **Merge & Split:** Combine multiple PDFs into one, or break a large document into smaller chunks.
* **Full Page Control:** Rotate pages that are upside down, delete unwanted pages, and drag to reorder them before saving.

## üõ†Ô∏è Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Adding Files
1. Open the **Doc & PDF** tool from the left sidebar.
2. Click the large **Add Files** upload area.
3. Select the PDFs or Images you want to work with from your device. 

### Step 2: Organizing & Editing
Once your files are loaded into the workspace, you will see a preview of all pages:
* **Reorder:** Click and drag any page to change its position in the final document.
* **Rotate:** Click the rotate icon on a specific page to fix its orientation.
* **Delete:** Click the trash icon to remove a page entirely.

### Step 3: Selection Mode (Advanced)
Need to extract only pages 2, 5, and 7? 
1. Toggle the **Selection Mode** switch on the left panel.
2. Click on the specific pages you want to keep or extract. Selected pages will be highlighted.

### Step 4: Exporting Your File
Once your document looks perfect, choose your export format:
* Click **Save PDF** to download the pages as a standard PDF file.
* Click **Save Images** to download a ZIP file containing the pages as individual image files.
