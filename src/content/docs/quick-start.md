Getting started with SuperApp takes less than 60 seconds. Whether you are on a laptop, tablet, or smartphone, SuperApp adapts to your screen and can be installed natively.

## üì± How to Install SuperApp (PWA)

For the best, fastest, and most app-like experience, we highly recommend installing SuperApp on your device. It takes almost zero storage space!

### On Android (Chrome / Edge)
1. Open SuperApp in your browser.
2. You will see a glowing **"Install App"** banner at the bottom of the screen.
3. Tap **Install**, and SuperApp will be added to your app drawer and home screen.

### On iOS (iPhone / iPad)
Apple's Safari requires a manual step:
1. Open SuperApp in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom.
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Confirm by tapping **Add**. You can now launch SuperApp directly from your home screen!

### On Desktop (Windows / Mac)
1. Open SuperApp in Chrome, Edge, or Brave.
2. Look at the right side of the URL address bar.
3. Click the **Install** icon (a monitor with a downward arrow) and confirm. 

> Using the installed app unlocks offline capabilities, smoother animations, and a distraction-free fullscreen mode.

## üîí Authentication & Account Setup

SuperApp uses a highly secure, modern authentication system backed by Appwrite infrastructure.

### 1. Creating an Account
* Click on the **Login / Sign Up** button.
* Enter your details. We use secure email verification to ensure your account is safe.
* Once verified, you instantly get access to your personalized dashboard and Cloud Storage.

### 2. Setting Up 2FA (Highly Recommended)
To protect your Cloud Storage and personal data, we strongly advise enabling Two-Factor Authentication (MFA).
1. Go to **Settings > Security**.
2. Click **Enable 2FA**.
3. Scan the generated QR code using an authenticator app (like Google Authenticator or Authy).
4. Enter the 6-digit code to verify. 
*Note: If you lose your 2FA device, you will need your backup codes to log in.*

### 3. Account Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)
We respect your data. If you choose to delete your account from the Settings menu, our automated cloud functions will permanently wipe **everything**:
* Your profile and active sessions.
* Your Cloud Storage files.
* Your Arcade scores, OTPs, and Search History.
* *Once deleted, this action cannot be undone.*
