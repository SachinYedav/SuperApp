### 1. Sticky Notes (Sticky Wall)

## üõ°Ô∏è Data Safety & Deletion Logic

We know how frustrating it is to accidentally delete a long-running habit streak or an important project note. To ensure absolute data safety, SuperApp uses a **Global Confirmation Modal** for all destructive actions.

**How Deletion Works:**
1. Click the delete/trash icon on any note, task, or habit.
2. A secure overlay (Modal) will appear on your screen, pausing your workflow.
3. You must actively **check the confirmation box** acknowledging the deletion.
4. Only then will the **Confirm** button become clickable. 

*This two-step verification guarantees that your hard-earned progress is never wiped out by a misclick.*
